About Us

APU Futsal Club is dedicated to promoting indoor football in a supportive and competitive environment. We train players, organize tournaments, and foster a love for futsal in the APU community.

🏆 Our Mission

To inspire passion for futsal, develop skilled players, and represent APU with excellence.

📅 What We Do

We organize weekly futsal matches, inter-university tournaments, and skills workshops.
