About Us

APU Marathon Club encourages runners of all levels to improve fitness, set personal goals, and participate in local and university-wide marathons. We focus on training, safety, and building a supportive running community.

🏆 Our Mission

To promote health, endurance, and teamwork through long-distance running.

📅 What We Do

We organize training sessions, university marathons, weekly runs, and endurance workshops.
